About Me

I am a graduate student at The Ohio State University. This blog/website documents my journey into the exciting world of Automotive Control Systems and Artificial Intelligence.

Currently, I am a Graduate Research Associate (GRA) at The Ohio State University Center for Automotive Research guided by Prof. Giorgio Rizzoni.

Biography: My career as an engineer started as a member of my undergraduate university's Formula SAE team as a freshman in 2012. I earned my Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2016 from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. I worked in the Intake/Exhaust design group in the Powertrain Engineering division of Honda R&D from July 2016 to July 2018. I joined The Ohio State University as a graduate student in 2018, working on projects with industry partners while working on my Masters Degree in Mechanical Engineering.
